## Physics infographic prompts (Singapore MOE O-Level, 2026)

Style: **flat vector / textbook diagram**, clean labels, no watermark, high-contrast, exam-revision friendly. Aspect ratio **16:9** (preferred). Dark background optional to match the app.

---

### Section I — Measurement

- **Topic 1 Physical Quantities, Units and Measurement**  
  Prompt: "Revision infographic: SI base units table (m, kg, s, A, K), common derived units (N, J, W, Pa) with unit breakdowns (e.g. N=kg·m/s²). Include a mini panel: scalar vs vector examples with arrows for vectors. Add a small measurement panel showing metre rule, vernier calipers, micrometer with least count labels and a 'zero error' callout. Flat vector, crisp typography, no photorealism."

### Section II — Newtonian Mechanics

- **Topic 2 Kinematics**  
  Prompt: "Kinematics poster: three graphs side-by-side (distance–time, velocity–time, acceleration–time) with annotations: gradient and area meanings. Include SUVAT equations block (v=u+at, s=ut+½at², v²=u²+2as). Add free-fall mini-panel with g=9.8 m/s² downward and a ball thrown upward showing v=0 at top but a=-g. Flat vector, clear axes and units."

- **Topic 3A Force and Pressure I**  
  Prompt: "Forces + density infographic: free-body diagram examples (block on surface, hanging mass, object in water with upthrust). Include W=mg, g in N/kg. Density section: ρ=m/V with measuring cylinder displacement method (V2−V1). Add 'floating vs sinking' with density comparison. Clean vector icons, minimal."

- **Topic 3B Force and Pressure II (Pressure)**  
  Prompt: "Pressure infographic: p=F/A with examples (knife, snowshoe). Fluid pressure: water tank with increasing pressure arrows with depth; dam thicker at bottom. Add hydraulics: two pistons with F1/A1=F2/A2 and arrows showing force multiplication. Flat schematic style, labelled."

- **Topic 4 Dynamics**  
  Prompt: "Newton's laws + resistive forces poster: three panels for 1st/2nd/3rd law with simple diagrams (trolley, force arrows). Include F=ma and momentum p=mv with impulse Ft=Δp. Add terminal velocity v–t graph curve leveling off with forces weight vs drag. Flat vector, exam-friendly."

- **Topic 5 Turning Effects of Forces**  
  Prompt: "Moments and equilibrium diagram: spanner on nut showing τ=F×d⊥; beam balanced on pivot with clockwise = anticlockwise moments. Include stability panel showing CoG and base of support (stable vs unstable). Add method for CoG of lamina (plumb line intersection). Clean vector."

- **Topic 6 Energy**  
  Prompt: "Energy revision poster: energy stores icons (KE, GPE, thermal, chemical). Equations: KE=½mv², GPE=mgh, work W=Fs, power P=W/t and P=VI. Include efficiency arrow (useful/total). Add energy transfer example: falling object (GPE→KE) and braking (KE→thermal)."

### Section III — Thermal Physics

- **Topic 7A Kinetic Particle Model I**  
  Prompt: "States of matter particle model: three panels solid/liquid/gas with particles and motion arrows; labels for spacing and motion. Include evaporation vs boiling comparison (surface vs throughout). Add diffusion illustration (perfume spreading). Flat vector, clear labels."

- **Topic 7B Kinetic Particle Model II (Internal Energy)**  
  Prompt: "Internal energy and heating curve: diagram showing internal energy = kinetic + potential. Heating curve with sloped sections and flat plateaus labelled 'latent heat of fusion' and 'latent heat of vaporisation'. Add steam vs hot water burn explanation (condensation releases latent heat)."

- **Topic 8 Thermal Processes**  
  Prompt: "Heat transfer poster: conduction (metal rod with particles/electrons), convection (convection currents in a room / beaker), radiation (IR waves from heater). Include surface colour effect: dull black absorbs/emits, shiny reflects. Add vacuum flask cross-section showing how each heat transfer is reduced."

### Section IV — Waves

- **Topic 9A General Properties of Waves I**  
  Prompt: "Wave properties infographic: labelled transverse wave (A, λ, crest/trough) and longitudinal wave (compression/rarefaction). Include v=fλ and f=1/T. Add 'in phase' markers one wavelength apart. Clean vector."

- **Topic 9B General Properties of Waves II (Sound)**  
  Prompt: "Sound infographic: longitudinal wave with compressions/rarefactions, pitch=f and loudness~amplitude. Include echo distance formula d=vt/2 with a simple echolocation diagram. Add ultrasound applications mini-panel (medical imaging)."

- **Topic 10 Electromagnetic Spectrum**  
  Prompt: "EM spectrum strip: radio→gamma with wavelength shrinking and frequency increasing arrows. Highlight visible band (red→violet). Add common uses per band (microwave cooking, IR remote, UV sterilisation, X-ray imaging, gamma radiotherapy). Include ionising vs non-ionising callout."

- **Topic 11A Light I (Reflection)**  
  Prompt: "Reflection diagram: plane mirror with normal, incident and reflected rays; i=r. Include plane mirror properties box (virtual, upright, same size, laterally inverted, same distance). Add periscope mini-schematic with two reflections."

- **Topic 11B Light II (Refraction)**  
  Prompt: "Refraction poster: ray entering glass block bending towards normal and leaving bending away. Include n=sin i/sin r and note frequency constant, speed and wavelength change. Add apparent depth diagram for object in water. Add dispersion prism splitting white light (violet bends more)."

- **Topic 11C Light III (Thin Converging Lenses)**  
  Prompt: "Converging lens ray diagrams: show principal axis, F and 2F. Provide 4 cases (object beyond 2F, at 2F, between F and 2F, inside F) with image properties labelled. Include 1/f=1/u+1/v and magnification m=v/u. Flat, clean."

### Section V — Electricity and Magnetism

- **Topic 12 Electric Charge and Current of Electricity**  
  Prompt: "Electric basics infographic: charge Q, current I=Q/t, p.d. V=W/Q, resistance R=V/I. Show conventional current vs electron flow directions. Include I–V characteristic mini-panels: ohmic resistor (straight line), filament lamp (curve), diode (one-way)."

- **Topic 13 D.C. Circuits**  
  Prompt: "Series vs parallel circuits comparison: circuit diagrams with ammeter/voltmeter placements; rules (series: same I, RT add; parallel: same V, current splits, 1/RT sum). Include a fuse-in-live-wire callout. Flat schematic."

- **Topic 14 Practical Electricity**  
  Prompt: "Household electricity safety poster: plug wiring (live, neutral, earth) with colours labelled; fuse location and purpose; dangers (shock, overheating). Include power/energy panel: P=VI, E=Pt, kWh conversion 1kWh=3.6×10⁶J. Add 'overloading socket' warning."

- **Topic 15 Magnetism and Electromagnetism**  
  Prompt: "Magnetism poster: bar magnet field lines N→S; compass aligning with field. Solenoid with right-hand grip rule; show strengthening by more turns/current/soft iron core. Motor effect: current-carrying conductor in magnetic field with Fleming’s left-hand rule labelled."

### Section VI — Radioactivity

- **Topic 16 Radioactivity**  
  Prompt: "Radioactivity infographic: atom diagram (p,n,e) with A and Z definitions. Three radiation panels α, β, γ with penetration (paper, aluminium, lead) and ionising strength. Include half-life graph (count rate halving) and safety triangle: time-distance-shielding. Add uses: smoke detector (α), thickness control (β), sterilisation/radiotherapy (γ)."
