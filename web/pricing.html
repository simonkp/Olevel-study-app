Pricing FAQ

Is this a subscription?

No. It's a one-time payment per subject (or for the bundle). You keep access until your O-Levels are done.

Can I upgrade from a single subject to the bundle later?

Email us — we'll discount the bundle by what you already paid. (Self-serve upgrade is on the roadmap.)

Refund policy?

Topic 1 of every subject is free, so there's a meaningful trial before you pay. If something genuinely doesn't work for you within 14 days, email and we'll refund.

Do parents need a separate account?

No. The same Google account opens both the student app and the parent dashboard.

I'm not in Singapore — does this still work?

Yes — the content is mapped to the Singapore-Cambridge O-Level syllabus, which is widely used. Pricing is in SGD; Stripe converts at checkout.
