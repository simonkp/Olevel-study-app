Privacy policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

LevelUp (“we”, “us”) builds a study hub for O-Level students. This page explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It is written for a PoC product — keep it honest and boring. No dark patterns, no ad networks, no selling of your data.

Who runs LevelUp

LevelUp is operated by an individual developer in Singapore as a pre-launch product. The contact email is hello@levelup.local.

Data we collect

  • Account: your Google account identifier (name, email, avatar URL) when you sign in with Google. We never see your Google password.
  • Study progress: XP ledger rows, topic mastery stats, quiz answer history, shop purchases/coupons — used to personalise your study and power the report.
  • Billing: if you buy a subject, Stripe processes the payment. We store your purchase reference and entitlement, but card details never touch our servers.
  • Diagnostics: minimal analytics events for page views and error signals. No cross-site tracking.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.
  • We do not profile minors for advertising.
  • We do not build behavioural-advertising audiences.

Data we share

We share data only with the sub-processors required to run the product: Supabase (database, auth, storage), Stripe (payments), Resend (transactional email), OpenAI / Gemini (only when you opt into an LLM feature, and only with the text of that single request).

How long we keep data

We keep study progress for the life of your account. If you ask us to delete your account, we delete your profile, entitlements, and identifiable progress within 30 days. Anonymised aggregates may stay in our analytics logs.

Your rights

You can request export or deletion of your account by emailing hello@levelup.local. Under the Singapore PDPA (and if you are in the EEA, the GDPR), you also have the right to object to processing or restrict it.

Cookies

We use a small number of first-party cookies / local storage entries to keep you signed in and remember your last subject. We do not use tracking cookies from advertising networks.

Children

LevelUp is intended for students 13 and older. If you are under 16 in the EEA or under 13 elsewhere, a parent must create the account and agree to this policy on your behalf.

Changes

If we materially change this policy we will update the date at the top of the page and, for existing customers, post a notice on the profile page before the change takes effect.
